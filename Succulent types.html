The lotus leaf is bright green, usually 0.5 - 4 cm long, depending on the age of the plant. The flowers are pink, small, growing at the tops of branches or axils of leaves. Shark jaw lotus originates from Zanzibar (an island country on the coast of Tanzania) which always has a dry climate all year round, so it has very good drought resistance. When the plant wilts, you just need to water it. Water will make the plant fresh again. The green shark-jaw lotus leaves grow around with sharp thorns that look "ferocious". The tree is suitable for people with strong personalities who do not give up before life's difficulties. Therefore, trees are often used as gifts for friends, colleagues, and relatives, accompanied by the message "try hard, failure only comes when we give up".

Conophytum bilobum Living pebble

Conophytum bilobum Living pebble has thick leaves, when it grows on the wall, it grows 2 leaves, one identical to the heart shape, with a dark green color, the tree looks very sturdy and has vitality. Heart-leaved succulents need light to photosynthesize, just like other succulents like early morning light. They can withstand direct sunlight, but you should not expose the plant to too much sunlight like the afternoon summer sun. The appropriate temperature is 15 - 35 degrees, so put the tree in a sheltered place but still ensure light like a balcony window, so it has sunlight and avoids rain and storms.
